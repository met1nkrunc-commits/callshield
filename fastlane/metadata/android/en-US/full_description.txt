Callshield is a free security app built specifically for Turkey, protecting you from fraud calls and spam SMS messages.

🛡️ WHAT IT BLOCKS
• Fake betting and gambling sites (bets10, 1xbet, vaycasino and 300+ sites)
• Legal threat SMS ("icra takibi" / debt enforcement scams)
• Phishing messages and suspicious short URLs
• Investment and crypto scams
• Fake delivery SMS ("your package is waiting")
• Prize and lottery scams

🔒 YOUR PRIVACY IS OUR PRIORITY
All analysis happens on your device. Your SMS content and personal information are never sent to any server.

⚡ HOW IT WORKS
1. Incoming SMS content is scanned locally on-device
2. Incoming calls are instantly checked against our database
3. Fraud number list auto-updates every 24 hours
4. You receive a notification when suspicious content is detected

📊 PREMIUM FEATURES
Real-time spam scoring via IPQS integration and detailed blocking statistics.

🇹🇷 Turkey-specific keyword analysis with a database of 10,000+ known fraud numbers.
